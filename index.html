Just Craftin'

Just Craftin' is a vanilla survival, whitelisted Minecraft server with crossplay! We're a small community of players who enjoying building and exploring what Minecraft has to offer. We require you to be at least 16 or older to apply. We are located in the United States. If this interests you, feel free to apply! We'd love to have you join the server!

If you're interested in supporting us, feel free to donate! If you donate at least $8, we'll color your username with any color. Also, please vote for the server! You receive 1 diamond for each vote. Upon reaching 20 votes, you will receive a custom emote with a custom color.

Before applying, please read our rules.
