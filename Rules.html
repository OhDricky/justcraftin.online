Rules

We are a trust-based server. Breaking any of these rules will result in being removed from the server.

In-Game Rules
1. Offensive language/actions are not allowed. 

Cursing is allowed if it is not aimed towards an individual.
Actions include buildings, gestures, or any other actions that could lead to offending someone. Please use your common sense!

2. Griefing and/or stealing is strictly prohibited.

This is a trust-based server, so these actions are not tolerated at all. Please be respectful of other people's items and buildings. If a building is clearly abandoned or destroyed with visible signs stating you can take/destroy stuff, you can do as you wish with discretion.  Please note that these kind of things are on a case-to-case basis.

3. Cheating and/or exploiting is not allowed.

Using any 3rd party clients that give a clear gameplay advantage or allow the use of cheats are not allowed. Clients such as LunarClient are allowed! Exploits of any kind are not allowed. However, TNT duping  (as long as it does not effect server performance) and access the Nether roof is allowed. If you are not sure about something, feel free to ask someone!

4. Spamming and/or advertising is not tolerated. 

Do not advertise of things not releated to the server, such as other servers or other services. Also, do not use real world currency for any services in-game. Please use common sense!

5. PvP is not allowed unless both parties agree.

This encompasses all PvP encounters. Do not raid or kill other players unless they give consent. Treat each other with the respect!

Discord Rules
1. Offensive language/actions are not allowed. 

Cursing is allowed if it is not aimed towards an individual.
Actions include emotes, gestures, or any other actions that could lead to offending someone. Please use your common sense!

2. Please use the correct channel.

Keep the subject of conversations related to the channel in which they are taking place.

3. Spamming and/or advertising is not tolerated. 

Do not advertise of things not releated to the server, such as other servers or other services. Also, do not use real world currency for any services in the Discord server. Also, abide by Discord's TOS.

4. NSFW posts and topics are not allowed in the server. 

Due to the server being available to people who are under the age of 18, NSFW topics and posts are not allowed on any channel of the Discord server. 

Innactivity:  If you are deemed to be innactive (not have joined the server for 10 days) without reason, you will be deemed innactive. If you are whitelisted to the server, but you do not join within 7 days, you will be deemed innactive. Innactive players are subject to removal at any point while they are innactive.

Discord is required in order to be apart of the server.  If you leave the Discord server, you will be removed from the whitelist.
